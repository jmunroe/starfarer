What you're seeing

  • 15,000+ real stars from the HYG v4.1 catalog (Hipparcos / Yale / Gliese), positioned in true 3D.
  • 5,200 confirmed exoplanets from the Open Exoplanet Catalogue. Cyan diamonds mark planet hosts.
  • Star colors and sizes are derived from measured color index and luminosity.
  • Open a planet system to see orbits and the star's estimated habitable zone.
